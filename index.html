Lenka Zviadadze (Lenka Z)

Lenka Zviadadze (Lenka Z) is an award-winning singer and actor from Tbilisi, Georgia. She has won multiple international music competitions and has gained national recognition through Georgia’s leading reality TV franchises, including Got Talent and The Voice of Georgia, where she earned a four-chair turn. Now based in New York City, she continues to build her artistic career across music, theatre, and performance.
